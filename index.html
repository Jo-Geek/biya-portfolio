About Me

An Electronic Engineer who followed her passion for 3D Modeling.

My main goal is to work as a 3D Modeling,Texturing and Lighting artist in Game Cinematics and Movies.

Since my childhood days I have always been fascinated by drawing and art. I did not know what the animation industry was back then so my main goal was to be a creator. My first encounter with 3D was with Blender. As a free software I explored it on my own and with the help of numerous tutorials. I realised I aspired to be 3D modeler ;one who could create worlds from a mere idea in my head. I went to VFS where I was professionally taught how to get around achieving that dream.
